ABOUT AZUREE FARM WORLD

The green arm of Azuree World Limited (A Kenyan enterprise founded by youth and women) was established to change youths’ narrative and perception of agri-business as an unattractive career of last resort, one of drudgery and low monetary benefits. Building on the principle of “leaving no one behind”, Azuree world is committed to contribute in creating a conducive environment for the realization of the youth, women and persons with disability engagement in Agricultural development initiatives and promoting equitable livelihoods. Their potential to contribute to the sector and indeed to the overall economic development is significant.
